Our restaurant has been ran and owned by the family for the past 50 years in our local community of Steamboat, Atlantis! We started when our founder Tim Timmy found inspiration for a new restaurant while walking down the local docks and finding his passion for restaurant service. After saving money for a few years and a lot of hard work you can see us here today!
